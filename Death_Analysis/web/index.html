Causes of Death

As global population increases, life expectancy rises, and living standards improve, causes of death across the world are changing. In this entry we present a global overview of the causes of death. The data visualisations and explainers in the early part of this entry attempt to provide a comparison between causes and risk factors of death across states and age groups. The sections which then follow explore the empirical data of specific causes in more detail.


Dataset for the analysis of death has taken from Indian gov website.The link is given in the resources section.
